Free Utilities iPhone & iPad iOS 17+

FormReady helps you spot likely missing proof, review readiness, and prepare paperwork with a calmer workflow. Bring PDFs, scans, screenshots, and image-based forms into one organized place—not only to fill and sign, but to understand what may still be missing, whether supporting proof might be required, and what to do next before you submit.

What you can expect

  • Missing Proof Predictor—suggestions for IDs, receipts, signatures, or attachments that may still be needed.
  • Submission Readiness—a simple view of how complete a form looks, not just whether fields are filled.
  • Import & organize—from Files, Photos, scans, and the share sheet into clear groups (e.g. To Complete, Needs Signature, Ready to Submit).
  • OCR & search—extract text when possible and find forms by title, date, issuer, or category.

Built for real paperwork

  • Admin, insurance, reimbursement, school, medical, claims, and general applications—anything that needs more than a quick signature.
  • Fill, annotate, and sign while keeping everything in one reassuring, human-friendly flow.
  • Status tracking: draft, needs work, ready to submit, submitted, waiting attachments, archived.
  • Core experience is fully free—essential tools are not locked behind a paywall.
